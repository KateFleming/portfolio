About

Hi I'm Kate. I'm a Seattle based web developer and designer, and an aspiring entrapreneur.

I've been an artist all my life, working mostly with paint, I figured it was time to step it up and work with a new medium. My desire to develop, build, and create has landed me right in the middle of the world of website design and development, and I couldn't be happier.

When I'm not messing around creating or learning code, you'll find me doing flips in the gym, hiking up a mountain, or writing a new piano piece. Check out my blog for some awesome pics.
